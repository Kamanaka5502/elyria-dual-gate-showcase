# Gate B — Execution Boundary

Gate B evaluates whether a proposed action may bind consequence.

## Evaluates

- authority
- admissibility
- state
- constraint
- capacity
- policy
- commit boundary readiness

## Outcomes

- EXECUTE — admissible and may bind
- REFUSE — unsafe or prohibited
- ESCALATE — requires human or higher authority
- REDIRECT — fixable before binding
- HALT — continuation is unsafe

## Boundary rule

Gate B is where consequence is decided.

No action becomes real without passing this gate.

## Private layer not exposed

- policy engine internals
- authority resolution logic
- thresholds
- runtime enforcement adapters
